# NyayAI
The platform functions as an AI-powered legal assistant designed to provide free and instant guidance for common legal issues in India. Users can describe their problems in plain language to receive actionable advice on matters such as handling unpaid wages or disputes over security deposits. Furthermore, the tool assists users in generating professional documents, including complaint letters, RTI applications, and legal notice templates that are ready for submission.

The application features a responsive user experience developed with a React-based frontend. The core logic is powered by AI models specifically trained on Indian laws, legal procedures, and government portals to ensure the information provided is both accurate and relevant.
